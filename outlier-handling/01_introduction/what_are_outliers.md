# What Are Outliers?

An **outlier** is a data point that significantly differs from other observations in a dataset.

### Example
Salaries:
25k, 28k, 30k, 27k, 5,00,000



Here, `5,00,000` is an outlier.

### Why Outliers Matter
- Affect mean and standard deviation
- Reduce model accuracy
- Can mislead analysis

### Causes of Outliers
- Data entry errors
- Measurement issues
- Natural rare events
- Different data distribution
