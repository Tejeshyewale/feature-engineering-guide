# Missing Indicator Method

The missing indicator method creates a new binary feature
to show whether a value was missing.

---

## How it works
- 1 → value was missing
- 0 → value was present

---

## Why use it
- Missingness may carry information
- Improves model performance

---

## Advantages
- Preserves missingness information
- Works well with other imputation methods

---

## Disadvantages
- Increases number of features

---

## One-liner
Missing indicator method adds a binary column to indicate missing values.
